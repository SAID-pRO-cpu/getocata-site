How it works

Three moves on every ticket. Zero auto-send.

A human always presses send. The model just makes sure the human gets there in 2 minutes instead of 8.

01 / categorize

Tag every ticket on arrival

Intent, priority, product area, and L1/L2/L3 routing — applied within seconds of the ticket landing. Tuned weekly on your real ticket history.

02 / draft

Pre-write the L1 reply

For every L1-eligible ticket, a first response sits in the agent's view ready to edit. Tone-matched to your existing macros. Nothing auto-sends, ever.

03 / flag

Surface the dangerous tickets

Churn-risk language, compliance triggers, payment failures, security keywords — flagged the moment they hit. Not three hours later when someone scrolls past it.

Built on top of your existing Zendesk / Intercom / HelpScout / Freshdesk via webhooks. No platform migration. No rip-and-replace. Removable in an afternoon if it doesn't work.

FAQ

Questions you'd ask.

How is this different from Zendesk AI Agents or Intercom Fin? +

Those are full agent-replacement plays at $50k+/year price points. Ocata sits on top of your existing helpdesk and makes your existing L1 team 30–50% faster. Different bet, different price, different implementation depth. Usually the right call for sub-500-person SaaS.

Who owns the prompts and data? +

You do. Full export on request. Prompts are tuned for your business — they're yours when the engagement ends.

Where does data live? +

Encrypted in-transit and at-rest. Postgres on Neon (US-east). SOC 2 in progress. No ticket data goes to third-party analytics. Anthropic's enterprise data controls give zero retention on Claude API calls.

Can I cancel the monthly? +

Yes — 30-day notice after the 6-month minimum. Or earlier if the 25% guarantee doesn't hit. Setup is non-refundable after we've done the work.

You're in Morocco — what about timezone? +

I work US hours for US clients. Casablanca is 4–7 hours ahead of US timezones depending on DST. Reply SLA is < 2 hours during US business hours for active clients.

Can I see it working before committing? +
